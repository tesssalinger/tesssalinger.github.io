Game Design and 3D Design

I have developed skills in 3D design focusing on modeling, UV unwrapping, rigging, weight painting, lighting, post-processing, and animation using Blender, Maya and Cinema 4D. I’m proficient in texturing both within 3D software and traditionally in Photoshop, as well as using Substance Painter to create texture, bump, and spec maps. Additionally, I have experience with Unity, where I create 3D virtual environments to integrate my models, utilizing C# in Visual Studio for object triggers and interactions.

Video Design

I am deeply passionate about video design and have years of experience enhancing my skills in After Effects and Premiere Pro allowing me to create a more efficient workflow. I’ve transitioned to other advanced software to bring my video projects to life, including Blender for overlaying 3D elements, Illustrator for crafting 2D motion graphics, and Nuke for compositing.
