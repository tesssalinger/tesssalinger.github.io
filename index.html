Tess Salinger is a Digital Designer based in Melbourne/Naarm, driven by a passion for visual storytelling. She finds interest in uncovering the beauty within everyday moments and translating them into captivating narratives. With expertise in Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro for video editing, Maya for 3D design, Illustrator for graphic design, and Unity for interactive experiences, navigating through these platforms to bring her creative visions to life. Immersing herself with a diverse range of digital tools and platforms, she excitingly explores the possibilities they offer. Her drive for growth motivates her to continually expand her skill set and explore new techniques within her craft. Collaboration is central to her approach, as she values the insights and expertise of her peers. Working alongside others fuels her creativity and enables her to refine her work with attention to detail. As a newcomer in the digital design world, she is determined to make her mark. With a unique blend of enthusiasm, creativity, and dedication, she is ready to carve out a meaningful path in the industry, leaving a lasting impact through her innovative and visually captivating work.
